🎨 Production Visual Polish (Anti-Gravity & Glass)
To match your 18 screenshots exactly, apply these "Micro-Interactions":

Staggered Loading: When the Dashboard opens, the KPI cards should float up one by one with a 0.1s delay (framer-motion's staggerChildren).

Glass Distortion: Charts should have a bg-white/10 and backdrop-blur-xl container. The grid lines of the charts should be semi-transparent white to maintain the "frosted" look.

The "Anti-Gravity" Pulse: Hovering over any bar in a chart causes that bar to glow and lift slightly (±4px), while a frosted tooltip appears with a "drop shadow" to simulate depth.
